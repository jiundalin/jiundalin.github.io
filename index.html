I am a political scientist studying how states, markets, and transnational actors govern the climate transition — with a focus on sustainable finance, Chinese politics, and East Asia. 我是政治學者，研究國家、市場與跨國行動者如何治理氣候轉型——聚焦於永續金融、中國政治與東亞。

I am an Assistant Professor at the Graduate Institute of National Development, National Taiwan University, a Faculty Affiliate of the Risk Society and Policy Research Center, and an Affiliate Researcher at the Taiwan Social Resilience Research Center. I received my Ph.D. in comparative politics and international relations from the Department of Government and Politics, University of Maryland, College Park. My research has appeared in Global Environmental Politics, Journal of Cleaner Production, The Pacific Review, and Journal of East Asian Studies, among others. 林竣達，國立臺灣大學國家發展研究所助理教授，臺大風險社會與政策研究中心研究員、臺灣韌性社會研究中心研究員。美國馬里蘭大學政治學博士（比較政治與國際關係），加州大學聖地牙哥分校國際關係與太平洋研究學院碩士。研究成果發表於 Global Environmental Politics、Journal of Cleaner Production、The Pacific Review、Journal of East Asian Studies、《人文及社會科學集刊》、《問題與研究》等期刊。

Research研究

My current projects examine the comparative politics and global governance of sustainable finance and artificial intelligence. An ongoing National Science and Technology Council project combines quantitative and qualitative methods to study the development of global sustainable finance, comparing Taiwan, Japan, South Korea, and China. 目前的研究聚焦於永續金融與人工智慧的比較政治與全球治理。進行中的國科會計畫以量化及質化方法，研究全球永續金融的發展，並著重臺灣、日本、韓國與中國的個案比較。

Advising學生指導

Thesis & Dissertation Advising論文指導

I welcome inquiries from students whose projects have a strong substantive fit with my research areas. Prospective advisees should have taken one of my courses that requires a final essay or research paper and earned a grade of A or higher. If you would like to work with me, please email me a concise description of your proposed research topic and an explanation of why I would be an appropriate advisor. I may have to decline requests when I am already advising too many students. 歡迎研究主題與我的研究領域高度契合的同學來信洽詢論文指導。欲申請指導的同學，應修習過我開設、以期末論文或研究報告為要求的課程，且成績達 A 以上。有意申請者，請以電子郵件簡述你的研究主題構想，並說明為何適合由我擔任指導教授。若指導學生人數已滿，我可能需要婉拒。

Letters of Recommendation推薦信

To request a letter, you should have completed one of my courses with a final essay or research paper and earned an A or higher, contacted me at least one month before the deadline, attended my office hours at least once, and waived your right to read the letter. Along with your request, please send the final paper from the course, a current resume or CV, an unofficial transcript, and a draft of your application essay or personal statement. Reminder emails a few days before the deadline are welcome. 申請推薦信者，應修習過我以期末論文或研究報告為要求的課程且成績達 A 以上、於截止日至少一個月前提出、曾參加過至少一次 office hours，並放棄閱讀推薦信的權利。來信時請一併提供：該課程的期末報告、最新履歷或 CV、非正式成績單，以及申請文件或自傳的草稿。截止日前幾天歡迎來信提醒。
